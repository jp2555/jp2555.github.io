Welcome! I am an Alexander von Humboldt (AvH) Postdoctoral Fellow at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT). My research aims at developing a new paradigm of data analysis technique that is high-dimensional and unbinned using deep learning.

Previously I was a research affiliate at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and a graduate research & teaching assistant at Yale University, where I developed deep learning-based algorithms for detector deconvolution/unfolding, and deployed them to perform precision measurements with the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider for my PhD study.

If you are a student looking for thesis topics, please feel free to reach out!
