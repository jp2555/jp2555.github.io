A search for the SM Higgs boson decaying to a pair of new spin-0 or spin-1 BSM particles, each subsequently decaying to a pair of leptons, in the four-lepton final state. The analysis uses the full Run 2 ATLAS dataset (139 fb$$^{-1}$$) at $$\sqrt{s}=13$$ TeV.

*(Part of doctoral dissertation work.)*
